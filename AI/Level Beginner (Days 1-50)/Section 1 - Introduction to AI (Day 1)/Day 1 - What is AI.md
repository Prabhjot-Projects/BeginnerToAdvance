
# ✅ **Day 1 – Foundations of AI (Strictly AI-Focused)**

## 🎯 Goal of Today

Build a **clear foundational understanding of what AI is**, why it matters, and what types of AI we’ll be working with as we progress through this roadmap.

---

## 🧠 What is AI — In the Context of This Roadmap

**Artificial Intelligence (AI)** refers to systems that can:

- **Learn** from data
- **Recognise** patterns
- **Make decisions** automatically

In this roadmap, AI is **not just automation or rule-based logic**. We're learning **machine learning**, **deep learning**, **natural language understanding**, and **reinforcement learning** — all subsets of AI where **data + algorithms** = intelligence.

---

## 🧩 3 AI Types — by Capability

| Type | Focus | Examples |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **ANI (Narrow)** | Performs one domain-specific task | Siri, ChatGPT, Google Translate |
| **AGI (General)** | Human-level intelligence | Not yet achieved |
| **ASI (Super)** | Surpasses human ability | Still hypothetical |

> 🔍 Even if Siri handles multiple tasks, it doesn’t think — it switches between hard-coded domains. That’s still ANI.

**✅ In this roadmap, we’ll be building ANI systems.**

Eventually, you’ll see what AGI could look like — but only after mastering data-driven ANI.

### **⚙️ Based on Functionalities (How the AI *works internally*):**

| **Type** | **Description** | **Examples** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Reactive Machines** | No memory; reacts only to current input | IBM’s Deep Blue |
| **Limited Memory** | Learns from historical data | Self-driving cars |
| **Theory of Mind** | Understands emotions and social interactions | Not yet achieved |
| **Self-Aware AI** | Has its own consciousness and self-awareness | Theoretical/futuristic |
